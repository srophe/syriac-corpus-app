    Xth Syriac Symposium VIIIth Conference of Christian Arabic Studies Granada 22-27 September 2008

    Ignacio Carbajosa

    [1] The Xth Syriac Symposium and the VIIIth conference on Christian Arab Studies will take place in Granada, Spain from Monday, September 22 to Saturday, September 27, 2008, hosted by the International Center for the Study of the Christian Orient.

    [2] The dead line for submitting communications is on the 15th of July 2008 and should be emailed to symposium08@icsco.org. (To allow for organizing the provisional program, a summary of a maximum of 10 lines was expected by the 30th April 2008.) The presentations should not exceed 20 minutes with an additional 10 minutes for questions.

    [3] For further information, visit http://www.icsco.org/simposio.html.

    Ignacio Carbajosa Facultad de Teología "San Dámaso" c/ Jerte, 10 28005 Madrid (Spain) +34 913644010
