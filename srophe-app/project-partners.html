Project Partners

Syriaca.org is a joint project of:

  • Vanderbilt University
  • Princeton University
  • Marquette University
  • Texas A&M University
  • New York University
  • Beth Mardutho Research Library

Current funding for the project is provided by the National Endowment for the Humanities and the International Balzan Prize Foundation. Past support has been provided by the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation and the University of Alabama.

Syriaca.org is also actively seeking additional institutional partners.
